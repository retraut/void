# Prepare Editorial Brief

### Shape

- `self`: synthesize the weekly review into a meeting-ready brief
- `prohibited`: hiding uncertainty or inflating confidence for tidy narrative

### Parameters

- `learning-summary`: patterns, hypotheses, and cautions from the performance
  diagnosis
- `next-action-queue`: ordered follow-up work for the next planning cycle

### Returns

- `learning-brief`: concise editorial summary with evidence highlights,
  caveats, decisions needed, and next actions

### Strategies

- Lead with what the team should change or preserve.
- Keep detail enough for trust, but short enough to read before a weekly
  planning meeting.
